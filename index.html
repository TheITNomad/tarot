A Gift Honed Over Decades

For over 35 years, I have walked the path between worlds reading the language of cards, stars, runes, and flame for those seeking clarity in love, life, and purpose. My gift was awakened early and has been refined through decades of dedicated practice and thousands of readings.

Married for nearly 40 years, I understand deeply the threads of relationships the joy, the complexity, the seasons of love. My readings carry not just mystical insight but the wisdom of lived experience. Whether you seek guidance on love, career, family, or your soul's greater journey, I am here to illuminate the path.

From the misty forests of the Pacific Northwest, I offer you a sacred space to receive the messages the universe has waiting for you.
